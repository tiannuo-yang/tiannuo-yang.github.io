Abstract

Hi there! I am a third-year master student at Nankai Baidu Joint Lab, College of Computer Science, Nankai University, with Prof. Yusen Li as my supervisor. I received the bachelor's degree from University of Science and Technology Beijing in 2022.

I work on System4ML and ML4System. As Moore's Law fades, my research aims to harness hardware resources, leverage ML to automate the operation of complex systems, and enhance the efficiency of Sys4ML (e.g., LLM and RAG). Click to see My CV.

Keywords: Machine learning system · Performance tuning · Resource isolation · Online learning algorithm · Retrieval augmented generation · Vector database
