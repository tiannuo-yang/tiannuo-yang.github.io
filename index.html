Hi there! I am an incoming PhD student at University of Southern California, advised by Willie Neiswanger. I work on System4AI & AI4System. I like to build low-cost, high-efficiency machine learning systems that serves a wide range of scenarios (e.g., LLM Agents). Some of them have been deployed in industry.
