Short Bio

Tiannuo Yang is a master student of NBJL, College of Computer Science, Nankai University, under the supervision of Prof. Yusen Li . He received the B.E. degree from University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB) in 2022. His research lies at the intersection of computer systems and machine learning – with a particular focus on automated performance tuning.
